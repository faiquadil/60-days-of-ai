## 💡 Key Learnings

- Effective prompts should be **clear, specific, and focused**.
- Providing **relevant context** greatly improves AI responses.
- Avoid unnecessary instructions, jokes, or unrelated requests.
- Define the expected output, constraints, and requirements explicitly.
- Precision is more valuable than lengthy prompts.
- Prompt optimization significantly enhances AI performance.
- Well-structured prompts produce cleaner and more reliable code generation.
