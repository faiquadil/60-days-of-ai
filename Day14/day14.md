# Day 14 - Job Red Flag Detector

## Overall Risk Score
88/100 (Very High Risk)

## Top Red Flags
- Junior role requiring 5+ years experience
- Excessive technology requirements
- 24/7 support expectation
- Hidden relocation requirement
- No salary disclosure
- Hustle culture indicators
- Long working hours reported

## Positive Signals
- Clearly defined tech stack
- Relocation disclosed upfront
- Broad technology exposure
- Potential ownership opportunities

## Final Verdict
Avoid (or proceed only with extreme caution)

## Key Learnings
- Job titles should align with experience requirements.
- Salary transparency is important.
- Remote jobs may contain hidden relocation clauses.
- Workplace culture phrases can reveal burnout risks.
- AI can help identify risks before applying.
