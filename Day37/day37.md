# Day 37 – Task Compass

## 📌 Overview

Today, I completed the **Task Compass** simulation, an interactive learning experience designed to explore how work flows through a real technology company. The activity focused on identifying task ownership, building efficient workflows, and understanding cross-functional collaboration across different workplace scenarios.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Understand task ownership within a technology company.
* Learn how work is routed between departments.
* Practice building efficient organizational workflows.
* Improve collaboration and decision-making skills.
* Analyze workplace performance through an Organizational Thinking Dashboard.

---

## 🏢 Workplace Selected

**Tech Company**

---

## 📚 Stages Completed

### ✅ Stage 1 – Who Owns This?

Identified the primary owner responsible for different workplace situations, including:

* Production incidents
* UX issues
* Feature requests
* Bug reports
* Security events
* Market shifts

**Focus:** Assigning clear ownership and accountability.

---

### ✅ Stage 2 – Task Routing

Created complete workflows showing how tasks move between teams.

Examples included:

* Customer support requests
* Feature ideas
* Security concerns
* Bug reports
* Performance issues

**Focus:** Understanding the correct sequence of work across departments.

---

### ✅ Stage 3 – Collaboration Challenge

Selected the departments that should collaborate during complex organizational events such as:

* Rapid business growth
* Product delivery delays
* Security vulnerabilities
* Public reputation issues

**Focus:** Cross-functional teamwork and coordinated decision-making.
