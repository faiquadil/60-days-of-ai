Day 8 - Build Your First AI-Powered Dashboard

Project Title

Personal Environmental Health Analyzer

Objective

Build an AI-powered dashboard using Claude that analyzes environmental health data and provides insights on air quality, water quality, health risks, and personalized recommendations.

What I Built

I created a Personal Environmental Health Analyzer dashboard using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. The dashboard allows users to:

View Air Quality Index (AQI) data for multiple cities
Analyze PM2.5 and PM10 pollution levels
Check water quality scores
Review environmental health impacts
Compare different cities
Receive personalized health recommendations
View environmental health report cards
