# Day 29 – Operation Lifeline: AI Supply Chain Crisis Simulation

## 📌 Objective

The objective of this project was to use AI to generate and run an interactive supply chain crisis management simulation. The simulation focused on decision-making under pressure, supplier negotiations, executive leadership, AI investments, and evaluating the final business performance.

---

## 🛠️ Tasks Completed

- Read the provided resources and watched the solution video.
- Opened Claude AI and set the effort level to **Low**.
- Generated the complete React HTML application using the Operation Lifeline prompt.
- Saved the generated HTML file.
- Ran the simulation in the browser.
- Reviewed the randomly generated company profile and supply chain crisis.
- Selected three strategic crisis response actions.
- Completed all supplier negotiation rounds.
- Answered CEO leadership questions.
- Selected two AI supply chain investments.
- Reviewed the executive dashboard and business performance.
- Replayed the simulation with different scenarios.
- Captured screenshots of the simulation and results.

---

## 🎯 Crisis Response Strategy

### Selected Response Actions
- Activate Backup Suppliers
- Reallocate Regional Inventory
- Proactive Customer Communication

### AI Investments
- Demand Forecasting

## 💡 Key Learnings

- Transparent leadership builds trust during a crisis.
- Early supplier diversification reduces supply chain disruptions.
- AI-powered demand forecasting improves business planning.
- Supplier risk monitoring helps identify potential disruptions before they occur.
- Customer communication is essential for maintaining trust during delays.
- Effective negotiation balances supplier relationships with business costs.
- Crisis preparedness is more valuable than reactive decision-making.
