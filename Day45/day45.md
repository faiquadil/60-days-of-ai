# Day 45 – AI Decision Coach

## Overview
Today, I built an AI-powered Decision Coach using Claude. The application guides users through a structured interview, analyzes their responses, and generates a comprehensive decision report with recommendations, risk analysis, and an actionable plan.

## Objective
To learn how AI can be used to support complex decision-making by transforming qualitative thoughts into a structured, data-driven evaluation.

## Features
- Interactive decision interview
- AI-generated decision report
- Decision matrix with scoring
- Assumption and cognitive bias analysis
- Premortem (failure scenario) analysis
- 7-day action plan
- Personalized recommendation
- Shareable summary cards

## My Decision Scenario
**Decision:** AI Application Development vs. MERN Stack Development

### Goal
Build a placement-ready portfolio that maximizes internship and career opportunities.

### Timeline
Make the decision within the next 1–2 weeks to stay focused on preparation.

## AI Verdict
**Recommended Path:** AI Application Development

### Why?
- Aligns with my current learning momentum.
- Enables faster development of real-world portfolio projects.
- Creates a more differentiated portfolio.
- Can still be combined with foundational MERN skills when needed.

## Key Learnings
- AI can simplify complex decision-making through structured conversations.
- Breaking a decision into measurable factors leads to better clarity.
- Identifying assumptions and biases improves objectivity.
- Premortem analysis helps anticipate risks before committing.
- A decision doesn't have to be permanent—it can evolve with experience.
