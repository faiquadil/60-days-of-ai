# Day 23 – Customer & MVP Blueprint

## Objective
Generate a Customer & MVP Blueprint for the AI Career Mentor startup using the Startup Validation Report and customer research. The goal was to identify the ideal customers, validate pain points, define the MVP, and create a roadmap for product development.

---

## Startup Name
**AI Career Mentor**

---

## Deliverables

- Executive Summary
- Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)
- Buyer Personas
- Top 10 Customer Pain Points
- Customer Journey Map
- Key Customer Objections
- Key Buying Triggers
- MVP Recommendation
- MoSCoW Prioritization
- Pricing Hypothesis
- Top 5 Risks
- 30-Day MVP Plan
- Founder Action Sheet
- Diagnostic Scores
- Final Verdict

---

## Key Learnings

- Identified the Ideal Customer Profile for students and educational institutions.
- Understood customer pain points related to career guidance and skill development.
- Learned how to map the complete customer journey from awareness to retention.
- Prioritized MVP features using the MoSCoW framework.
- Designed a pricing hypothesis for both B2C and B2B customers.
- Identified major business risks and mitigation strategies.
- Created a structured 30-day MVP execution plan.
- Defined actionable next steps for building and validating the startup.
