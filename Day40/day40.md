# Day 40 – AI Assistant Builder (ATS Resume Scorer & Optimizer)

## Objective
Built an AI-powered ATS Resume Scorer & Optimizer using Claude AI Assistant Builder. The assistant analyzes resumes against a target job description and provides an ATS score, keyword analysis, improvement suggestions, and resume rewrite recommendations.

---

## What I Built
**Project Name:** ResumeReady – ATS Resume Scorer & Optimizer

The application allows users to:
- Upload or paste resume content.
- Paste a target job description.
- Analyze resume compatibility with the job description.
- Generate an ATS score.
- Identify missing keywords.
- Receive structured improvement suggestions.
- View rewritten resume bullet points for stronger impact.

---

- Resume upload/paste support
