## Key Operational Challenges

During the simulation, I managed several real-world supply chain disruptions, including:

* Weather Disruptions
* Port Congestion
* Demand Spikes
* Factory Machine Failures
* Wrong Inventory Counts
* Supplier Delays
* Warehouse Stock Shortages
* Truck Breakdowns
* Damaged Shipments
* Transportation Delays

Each incident required rapid prioritization and selecting the most effective corrective action before the alert timer expired.

---

## Key Learnings

* Real-time visibility is critical for effective supply chain management.
* Prioritizing high-impact incidents first significantly improves operational performance.
* Fast, data-driven decisions reduce operational risk and maintain service levels.
* Inventory accuracy directly impacts customer satisfaction and order fulfillment.
* Diversified transportation and supplier strategies improve overall supply chain resilience.
* AI-powered operational dashboards enable proactive decision-making during disruptions.
